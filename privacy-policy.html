🔒 Privacy Policy

NetRoute - Smart Proxy & Network Routing

Last Updated: May 27, 2026

🛡️ Our Privacy Commitment

NetRoute does NOT collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data.

All information you enter into the app (proxy configurations, routing rules, settings) is stored locally on your device only. We have zero access to your data.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

NetRoute does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Proxy server credentials or configurations
  • Browsing history or network activity
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data (except for optional background monitoring)
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data

Zero Data Collection: We cannot access your data because we never collect it in the first place.

2. Local Data Storage

All data in NetRoute is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms:

  • SwiftData: Proxy profiles, routing rules, and connection logs
  • UserDefaults: App settings and preferences
  • Keychain: Sensitive credentials (if provided)

This data is protected by your device's encryption and is only accessible to NetRoute on your device. It is never transmitted to any servers or third parties.

3. No Notifications

NetRoute does NOT use any form of notifications - neither local notifications nor remote push notifications.

  • No local notifications
  • No push notifications
  • No notification servers
  • No notification data collection

The app operates silently without sending you any alerts or notifications.

4. Location Services (Optional)

NetRoute requests location permissions for optional background monitoring:

  • Purpose: To track network connectivity status in the background
  • Usage: Location data is used only to keep the app active in the background
  • Storage: Location data is NOT stored or logged
  • Transmission: Location data is NEVER sent to any servers

You can choose to deny location access - the app will still function normally but background monitoring will be limited.

5. Third-Party Services

NetRoute does NOT use any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Social media SDKs
  • Cloud storage services

The app is completely self-contained and does not communicate with any external servers (except the proxy servers you configure yourself).

6. Proxy Connections

When you connect to a proxy server through NetRoute:

  • Your device connects directly to the proxy server you specified
  • NetRoute acts as a configuration tool only
  • We do NOT operate, control, or have access to your proxy servers
  • All network traffic goes through YOUR proxy server, not ours

Important: Your proxy server provider may collect data according to their own privacy policy. NetRoute has no control over or access to any data collected by proxy servers.

7. Data Security

Although we don't collect data, we take security seriously:

  • All local data is encrypted by iOS
  • Credentials are stored in iOS Keychain (most secure storage)
  • App follows Apple's security best practices
  • Regular security updates via App Store

8. Children's Privacy

NetRoute is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from users of any age, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, there are no special provisions needed for children's privacy.

9. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Delete individual items: Swipe to delete profiles, rules, or logs in the app
  • Clear all data: Delete the app from your device
  • Reset settings: Use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings (if available)

When you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date
  • Announced in app update notes (for significant changes)
  • Effective immediately upon posting

We will never introduce data collection without explicit user consent and clear notification in advance.

11. Your Rights

Since NetRoute does not collect any data:

  • Right to Access: All your data is on your device - you have full access
  • Right to Delete: Delete the app to remove all data
  • Right to Portability: Export not needed - data never leaves your device
  • Right to Object: Not applicable - no data collection occurs

13. Compliance

NetRoute's privacy practices comply with:

  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA (California, USA)
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

Since we don't collect data, compliance is straightforward - there's no data to protect, share, or misuse.
